About

This calculator helps optimize vancomycin dosing for adult patients while predicting drug concentrations. The calculator follows current clinical guidelines focusing on AUC-guided dosing, with target AUC/MIC ratios of 400-600 mg*h/L. Recommendations are generated using established pharmacokinetic equations, including the Cockcroft-Gault and Matzke equations.

*Disclaimer: This calculator serves as an educational resource for healthcare professionals only. It should not replace clinical judgment, formal dosing guidelines, or professional medical evaluation and treatment decisions.
